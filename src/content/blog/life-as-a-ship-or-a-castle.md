## Ships

-   Exploratory, seeking new experiences
-   Choose a destination or mission
-   Adapt to changing conditions
-   Refit equipment depending on the environment

## Castles

-   Seeking to establish oneself in place
-   Choose a location or mindset
-   Resist changing conditions
-   Build on top of existing structure
