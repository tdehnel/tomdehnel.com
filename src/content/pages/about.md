## SEO Consulting

I've worked in marketing since 2005. I'm old enough that we used to call what I do "digital marketing" when I started. I look young because I sleep upside down. Just joking that's an old picture and I sleep in a regular horizontal bed. I've done SEO for companies from pre-launch startups to major tech and finance firms to really big e-commerce sites. I like what I do. It still feels like magic. I like to question assumptions. I focus on what corporate people call "leveraged solutions." In English, that means getting the most bang-for-your-buck. SEO can be confusing because there are always a million things you can do. The trick is knowing which ones will produce the best business results. I have high openness and a bias towards action, so I usually err on the side of getting something up and running quickly so we can test it. I prefer to say how I feel directly. I _can_ move slow and build consensus, but it takes extra effort. To me, it makes sense to launch fast, learn, and continually improve. I think [Edward Deming](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/W._Edwards_Deming) was a G. I'm particularly good at explaining things to different types of people. I can communicate SEO equally well to founders, executives, managers, individual contributors, developers, creatives, and probably your grandparents. If you're interested in learning more about me or my SEO services, contact me [via LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tdehnel/).
