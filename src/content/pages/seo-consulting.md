Why bother creating another 800 word blog post if it's not driving your business?

I think it’s a mistake to recommend a Technical Audit every time. But if there are issues that are hurting Google’s ability to crawl and index your site, that’s low-hanging fruit that needs to be fixed.

Whether it’s problems with page speed, site architecture, navigation structure, sitemaps, or meta tags, my technical SEO audits will help your dev team focus on the issues that need to be fixed now.
