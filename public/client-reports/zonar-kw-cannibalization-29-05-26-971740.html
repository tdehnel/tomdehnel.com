Executive summary

Keyword cannibalization happens when more than one page on your site competes for the same Google search, splitting clicks and ranking signals between URLs that should be working together. This dilutes authority and can hold back the page best suited to win. Below are the scenarios we recommend addressing, with a specific action for each.
